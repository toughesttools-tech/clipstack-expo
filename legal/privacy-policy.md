# Privacy Policy — ClipStack

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

ToughestTools Tech ("we," "us," or "our") operates ClipStack. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

**Data stored on your device only:**
- Clipboard history (text you copy while using ClipStack)
- Pinned clips and categories
- App preferences and settings

**Important:** Clipboard contents never leave your device. All data is stored locally only.

**Data collected by third parties:**
- **RevenueCat** collects purchase transaction data (subscription status, purchase date, product ID) to validate your in-app purchases.

We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- Clipboard data stays on your device in local storage — period
- We never transmit clipboard contents anywhere
- RevenueCat data is used solely to validate your Pro subscription
- No data is ever sent to our servers

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| RevenueCat | In-app purchase validation | https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy |

---

## 4. Data Retention

Your clipboard history is stored on your device until you clear it or delete the app. We do not retain any data.

---

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access:** Your data is on your device — open the app to see it
- **Delete:** Clear history in-app or delete the app entirely
- **Control:** You can disable clipboard access in iOS Settings at any time

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

ClipStack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
